Web Development

Web development is the process of creating, building, and maintaining websites and web applications that run on the internet. It involves various tasks like designing the user interface (how a website looks), coding the functionality (how a website works), and ensuring the website operates efficiently and securely.

1. In real life, web development plays a crucial role in almost every aspect of modern life. Here are a few examples of its applications:

2. E-Commerce: Online shopping platforms like Amazon or Flipkart rely on web development to create user-friendly websites where customers can browse and purchase products.

3. Education: Websites for online learning platforms like Coursera or edX help people access educational resources from anywhere in the world.

4. Communication: Social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram) and email services are all products of web development

5. Healthcare: Many hospitals and clinics have websites or web portals where patients can book appointments, access medical records, or consult doctors online.

6. Entertainment: Streaming services like Netflix and YouTube are examples of web development enabling access to movies, music, and videos.

7. Business Operations: Companies build websites for branding, marketing, and providing services to customers, while internal web apps help manage operations.
